I'm an Assistant Professor and Senior Researcher at Oregon State University's Applied Economics Department. My focuses are environmental economics, development economics, energy, sustainability, program evaluation, climate change, entrepreneurship.

My Ph.D. in Applied Economics and Management is from the Dyson School of Applied Economics and Management, Cornell University, 2015.
